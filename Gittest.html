About Me

      I recently graduated from secondary school, and I'm starting my first year in the real world. I make music, mostly hip-hop, but once in a while, I make beats for other genres. During the 6 months I was off-school, I saved up to buy a studio. I now own a low-end professional studio, which has served wonders for me. Music can be frustating. Sometimes it can be hard to get an end result, and I find often what I set out to do is never the same as the end product. In a way, it is similiar to programming. For example, when making this website, I was initially set on having each section be its' own color, but I couldn't figure out how to get rid of the white space in between the blocks. Eventually, I decided to group all the semantic elements for each section into a div element to create one huge block. I'm excited to see how much I will improve at creating web pages. I am certain I will be able to make what I initially set out to make very soon.
